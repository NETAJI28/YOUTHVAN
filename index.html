Welcome to the Student Union Website: Connecting Students, Empowering Lives

Our mission is to create a platform where students in Indore can seek help, support, and opportunities for personal and academic growth.

Through our website, students can reach out to us by sending messages, videos, and photos, allowing us to understand their challenges and provide assistance.

Helping Students in Need

Our website serves as a platform for students in Indore to seek assistance when facing challenges in their academic or personal lives. Students can send messages, videos, and photos through our website to communicate their concerns effectively. This enables our student union group, छात्रसंघ NSUI, to understand their problems and provide necessary support.

Student Union Group: छात्रसंघ NSUI

Our team of dedicated student representatives aims to address student issues, advocate for their rights, and promote their welfare. With a team of passionate individuals, NSUI works tirelessly to ensure students' voices are heard and their needs are met.

Collaborations

NSUI Congress: Collaborating to amplify our impact at a broader level.

Mawale Welfare Foundation: Partnering to extend our support network and provide comprehensive assistance.

Together, we strive to create a conducive environment for students to thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Join and Make a Difference

We invite students to join our team and actively contribute to social work, leadership development, and community initiatives. Together, we can make a positive impact on the lives of students in Indore.
